Volume Indicators

OBV

On-Balance Volume

Cumulative buying/selling pressure indicator

  • Rising OBV + Rising Price = Strong uptrend
  • Falling OBV + Rising Price = Weak trend
  • Divergence = Potential reversal

VWAP

Volume Weighted Average Price

Average price weighted by volume

  • Price > VWAP = Bullish bias
  • Price < VWAP = Bearish bias
  • Acts as dynamic support/resistance

Volume Ratio

Current Volume / SMA Volume

Compares current to average volume

  • >150% = Very high volume
  • 100-150% = Above average
  • <50% = Very low volume

PVT

Price Volume Trend

Volume-weighted momentum indicator

  • Rising PVT = Accumulation
  • Falling PVT = Distribution
  • Divergence = Reversal signal
